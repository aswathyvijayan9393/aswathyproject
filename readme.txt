Project name:Website for Agriculture office,Ottasekharamangalam
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   The website has been developed for Agriculture office,Ottasekharamangalam.Itis a online website.This system have three modules they are admin,user and
staff.Farmers are the users,Agriculture officer is administrator.The users can registerin to this site.Users are farmers.Admin as an agriculture officer.Admin home is admin.php page.User home is userhome.php.

---------------
